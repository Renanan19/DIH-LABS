Mastering Golf Swing Tempo with AI

Every great golfer, from Tiger Woods to Nelly Korda, has a distinct but consistent tempo. Most professionals aim for a 3:1 ratio — meaning the backswing takes exactly three times longer than the downswing.

Why Tempo is the Secret to Consistency

When your tempo is off, your timing suffers. A rushed downswing often leads to a slice or a hook because the clubface doesn't have time to square up. Achieving a consistent 3:1 ratio ensures that your body and club are in sync at the moment of impact.

How SmartCaddie AI Tracks Your Timing

Using high-speed computer vision, SmartCaddie identifies the exact frame your takeaway starts, the peak of your backswing (P4), and the moment of impact (P7). The app then calculates your ratio automatically.

  • The Backswing: SmartCaddie measures the duration from P1 to P4.
  • The Downswing: Measures from P4 to P7.
  • The Ratio: Instantly displays if you are at 3.0:1, 2.5:1, or rushing the shot.
