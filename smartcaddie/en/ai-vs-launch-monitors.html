AI vs. Launch Monitors

In the world of golf tech, names like Trackman and GCQuad are the gold standard. They use radar or high-speed cameras to track the ball. But for most golfers, AI-powered swing analysis is becoming the smarter choice for daily practice.

Hardware vs. Software

Launch monitors focus on the Result (Ball flight, spin, speed). AI swing analyzers like SmartCaddie focus on the Cause (Body movement, swing path, plane). You can have a great ball flight with a "compensating" swing that eventually leads to injury or inconsistency.

Why AI is winning for amateurs:

  • Accessibility: No $20,000 investment. Just your smartphone.
  • Visual Feedback: Seeing your swing path overlaid on video is more intuitive than reading a spreadsheet of numbers.
  • Portability: Practice anywhere — range, backyard, or indoor net.

The Hybrid Approach

The best pros use both. They use radar to dial in their distances and AI to monitor their "Positions" (P1-P10). SmartCaddie provides that professional-level biomechanical feedback for free, right in your pocket.
