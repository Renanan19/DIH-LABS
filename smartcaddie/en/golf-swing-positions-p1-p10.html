The 10 Golf Swing Positions (P-System)

In modern golf instruction, the "P-System" is the gold standard for breaking down the swing into measurable, comparable segments. SmartCaddie AI uses these 10 positions to analyze your mechanics and provide precise feedback.

The Backswing Phase

P1: Address

Initial setup and ball position.

P2: Shaft Parallel

Club shaft is parallel to the ground in the takeaway.

P3: Arm Parallel

Lead arm is parallel to the ground.

P4: Top of Swing

Transition point between backswing and downswing.

The Downswing & Impact

P5: Arm Parallel

First stage of the downswing.

P6: Shaft Parallel

Delivery position before impact.

P7: Impact

The moment of truth: club meets ball.

The Follow Through

P8: Shaft Parallel

Post-impact extension.

P9: Arm Parallel

Folding of the arms in the release.

P10: Finish

Balanced completion of the swing.

Why Benchmarking Matters

By identifying these positions automatically, SmartCaddie can compare your "P4" (Top) to that of a professional. Are your hands too high? Is your shaft cross-line? AI detects these deviations in milliseconds, allowing you to focus on one position at a time during your practice session.
