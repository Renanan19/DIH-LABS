IPTV Setup Guide

Learn how to get the most out of StreamVision in seconds.

M3U URL Configuration

The M3U format is the most common format for IPTV playlists. Here is how to use it:

  • 1. Copy your URL: Get the link provided by your service provider.
  • 2. Add in App: Click the "+" button in the top right corner.
  • 3. Name your list: Give it a name (e.g., "My TV") to find it easily.
  • 4. Enjoy: StreamVision downloads and organizes your channels instantly.

Xtream Codes Configuration

The Xtream Codes API offers better management of Movies and Series categories.

  • Server: The URL address (e.g., http://server-name.com:8080).
  • Credentials: Enter your Username and Password.
  • Auto-Sync: StreamVision automatically syncs posters and summaries.
