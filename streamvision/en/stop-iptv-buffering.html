How to Stop IPTV Buffering

Nothing ruins a movie night or a live sports match like the dreaded spinning circle. Buffering is the most common issue in IPTV, but most of the time, it's fixable.

1. Check Your Internet Speed

For HD streaming, you need at least 15 Mbps. For 4K, aim for 25-50 Mbps. Use a speed test app to confirm you're getting what you pay for.

2. Use a Wired Connection (Ethernet)

Wi-Fi is convenient but unstable. If you're streaming on a smart TV or box, try using an Ethernet cable for a much more stable data flow.

3. ISP Throttling & VPNs

Sometimes, your Internet Service Provider (ISP) detects high-bandwidth video streams and slows them down on purpose. A high-quality VPN can prevent this "throttling."

4. Player Optimization

StreamVision is optimized for low-latency playback. Ensure you are using the latest version of the app to benefit from our hardware acceleration and buffer management updates.
